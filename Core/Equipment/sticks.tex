\section{Point Sticks}\label{core:sec:sticks}

In order to count your score, add up the values of all sticks in your point tray, then if you are playing with bust sticks, subtract the value of the bust sticks you started with. So if you are playing with the distribution in Figure~\ref{core:fig:13stick}, and you have one of each stick left in your tray, you have: \(10,000 + 10,000 + 5,000 + 1,000 + 500 + 100 - 20,000 = 6,600 \text{ points}\).
